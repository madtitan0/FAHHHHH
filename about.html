Our Story

Building Hosur's Most Trusted Finance Institution

FA – the Finance Academy was founded on a simple yet powerful belief: that quality finance education should not be a privilege of metropolitan cities alone. We began in Hosur with a small batch of CA Foundation students, a handful of dedicated tutors, and an uncompromising commitment to excellence.

Over the years, we transformed from a modest coaching centre into a full-fledged finance institution — complete with dedicated computer labs equipped with professional financial software, a curated library of industry literature, and our own in-house publishing unit that produces the study material used by every FA student.

Today, FA stands as Hosur's benchmark for finance education. With 26 programs spanning 9 learning modes, we serve students from every background — school leavers preparing for CA Foundation, working professionals seeking certification, and lifelong learners exploring finance for the first time. Over 500 students have passed through our doors, and more than 100 have emerged as rank holders, state toppers, and distinction achievers.
